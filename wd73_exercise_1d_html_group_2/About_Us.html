About Us

Welcome to JB Travel Agency! With years of experience and a passion for exploration, we are here to help you discover the world's most captivating destinations. Our knowledgeable team is dedicated to curating unforgettable experiences for every traveler. As you embark on your journey with us, let's take a brief trip through the history of our esteemed travel agency. Established in 2020, JB Travel Agency was founded by Mr. Jonathan Pomar and sir BG, an avid travelers and entrepreneurs. They envisioned a company that would cater to the diverse travel needs of individuals and families alike, offering personalized services and expert guidance. From the very beginning, our agency focused on creating unique itineraries, connecting travelers with immersive cultural experiences, and fostering a love for global exploration.

In the early years, JB Travel Agency primarily served local clientele, arranging domestic trips and tours within the United States. However, as the demand for international travel grew, we expanded our services to encompass a wide range of global destinations. With our expanding network of partners and contacts worldwide, we began curating bespoke vacations that catered to the unique preferences and interests of our clients. Throughout the 1990s, JB Travel Agency thrived, earning a reputation for excellence and customer satisfaction. Our team of travel experts continuously traveled the globe, seeking out hidden gems and off-the-beaten-path destinations to offer our clients an unparalleled travel experience. We built strong relationships with airlines, hotels, and local tour operators, ensuring that our customers received the best value and the highest standards of service.

In the early 2000s, as technology advanced and the internet became more accessible, JB Travel Agency embraced the digital era. We launched our user-friendly website, allowing travelers to explore destinations, browse itineraries, and make bookings online. This transition enabled us to reach a wider audience, and our customer base expanded significantly. In recent years, JB Travel Agency has embraced sustainable travel practices and responsible tourism. We prioritize working with eco-friendly accommodations, promoting local communities, and supporting initiatives that preserve the environment and cultural heritage. Our commitment to sustainable travel allows us to create meaningful experiences while minimizing our impact on the destinations we visit.

Today, JB Travel Agency stands as a trusted name in the travel industry, known for our expertise, personalized service, and attention to detail. We offer a wide array of travel options, including luxury vacations, adventure trips, family getaways, and group tours. Our team of dedicated travel consultants is passionate about crafting unforgettable journeys that cater to your unique preferences and aspirations. Whether you dream of exploring ancient ruins, relaxing on pristine beaches, embarking on thrilling safaris, or immersing yourself in vibrant cities, JB Travel Agency is here to turn your travel dreams into reality. With our extensive knowledge, global connections, and unwavering commitment to exceptional service, we are your gateway to the world.

Come, join us on a journey of a lifetime!
